Our Story

Welcome to Cafe House, your cozy haven in the heart of Siliguri. As a proud Bengali cafe, we blend tradition with modern comfort, offering delicious coffee,refreshing mocktails,savory snacks,and an array of traditional Bengali treats perfect for any time of the day.

At Cafe House, we believe in creating a warm, inviting atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

Thank you for being a part of our story. We hope you find a little bit of magic here at Cafe House.
